How to Play Lab Breakout

Objective: Investigate the lab breach, collect clues, interrogate suspects, and escape before time runs out.

Controls:

  • Click "Play" to start the cutscene and begin the game.
  • During the cutscene, use "Next" to advance or "Skip to Interrogation" to jump ahead.
  • In the interrogation phase, type commands or suspect IDs (S1-S5) to question people.
  • You have 5 minutes to solve the case!

Suspects:

  • S1: Dr. Evelyn Kallen (Microbiologist)
  • S2: Dr. Marcus Reed (Bioinformatician)
  • S3: Dr. Lucia Chen (Senior Lab Technician)
  • S4: Dr. Samuel Ortiz (Quality Control Analyst)
  • S5: Dr. Penelope Grant (Sterile Area Technician)

Good luck, detective!
