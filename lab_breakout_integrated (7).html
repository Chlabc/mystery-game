How to Play Lab Outbreak

Objective: Interrogate the five scientists and work out who caused the breach.

Flow:

  • Click Play to enter the interrogation console.
  • Select a suspect from the left panel.
  • Ask questions in the chat input, or use the quick prompt chips.
  • When you are ready, type ACCUSE Dr. Full Name.
  • You have five minutes to accuse the correct scientist before the culprit escapes.

Suspects:

  • Dr. Evelyn Kallen — Microbiologist
  • Dr. Marcus Reed — Bioinformatician
  • Dr. Lucia Chen — Senior Lab Technician
  • Dr. Samuel Ortiz — Quality Control Analyst
  • Dr. Penelope Grant — Sterile Area Technician

The guilty suspect will sound plausible, but under pressure their details may wobble.
